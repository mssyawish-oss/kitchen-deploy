✏️ Correct the AI — edit its instructions
Each box is exactly what the AI is told to look for. Tap one to open it, tweak the wording (e.g. add a rule like "never count a row off shelves 4–6"), then Save — it applies to every read from then on. This is the simple way to correct it. Reset to default undoes your edits.
🔬 AI Debug — what the camera sees on every check
A live trace of every analysis: the exact frame, the AI's raw reply, what we read per shelf, and the decision the logic made — so you can see precisely where it's going wrong and what to adjust.
● LIVE
Recent frames (~½s apart) — tap the exact one where a row came off to correct it:
🤖 Auto-AI captures — every row the camera counted off by itself
Each time the AI decides a row came off, it records a full trace at that exact moment (frame 1) and then re-reads the scene continuously (a fresh read every ~2–3 seconds) for ~30 seconds after — each frame shows what it saw and what it did, just like the live trace, and each can be corrected. These are saved on the server and survive restarts (most recent 20 events). Every correction you make — here or on the live trace — is fed back into every future read.
